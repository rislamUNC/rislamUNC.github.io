Welcome to Red Paws Reserve. Our mission is to Rescue, Rehabilitate, and Replenish the global population of Red Pandas. We are a nonprofit, nonexistent, student founded organization dedicated to helping a species that is nearly extinct.


Our staff is comprised of experts, volunteers, and enthusiants that provide around the clock care for these beautiful creatures. We also provide long-term care for animals that cannot be released back into their natural environment.


Will you support us? We need caring volunteers and donors to help care for these animals. You can also contact us to become a member of the Red Paws Family, or an official sponsor. Contact us today!
