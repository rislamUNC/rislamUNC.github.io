Who Are We

OUR MISSION

Red Paws Reservation is committed to the conservation of wild red pandas and their habitat through the education and empowerment of local communities.

OUR VISION

We envision secure, viable populations of wild red pandas thriving throughout their historic range. Preservation of this species and its habitat benefits the region and, as a result, it is valued and protected by all.

Conservation

Conservation is the cornerstone of our work. We protect and restore red panda habitats all around the world.

It all started in Chapel Hill, NC where we started establishing the Red Paws Reservation. Now this community-protected wildlife corridor is being expanded to remaining red panda areas across the globe.
